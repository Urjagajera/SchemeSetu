Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Insurance Agriculture Central Sector

Full Description

The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is a government-sponsored crop insurance scheme that integrates multiple stakeholders on a single platform. Launched in 2016, it aims to support sustainable production in the agriculture sector by providing financial support to farmers suffering from crop loss or damage arising out of unforeseen events.

The scheme provides comprehensive insurance cover against failure of the crop, thus helping in stabilizing the income of the farmers and encouraging them to adopt innovative and modern agricultural practices.

verified Eligibility Criteria

  • check_circle All farmers including sharecroppers and tenant farmers growing the notified crops in the notified areas are eligible for coverage.
  • check_circle Mandatory coverage for all farmers who have sanctioned credit limits or have availed crop loans from Financial Institutions for the notified crops.
  • check_circle Optional coverage for non-loanee farmers.
  • check_circle Aadhar card is mandatory for application.

Scheme Benefits

payments

Financial Support

Covers yield losses for food crops, oilseeds, and annual commercial/horticultural crops.

history_edu

Low Premium

Uniform premium of only 2% to be paid by farmers for all Kharif crops and 1.5% for Rabi crops.

satellite_alt

Smart Assessment

Uses remote sensing, smartphones, and drones for quick crop loss estimation and claim settlement.

security

Post-Harvest Coverage

Coverage available up to a maximum period of two weeks from harvesting for unseasonal rains.

Application Process

1

Registration

Register on the PMFBY portal using your mobile number and Aadhar details.

2

Crop Selection

Select the state, district, bank branch, and the crop you wish to insure.

3

Upload Documents

Digitally upload all required documents mentioned in the documents section.

4

Premium Payment

Pay the calculated premium amount via Net Banking, Debit Card, or UPI.

5

Application Tracking

Receive an Application ID to track the status of your insurance policy.

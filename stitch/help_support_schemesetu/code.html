How can we help you today?

Find answers to common questions about government schemes, eligibility, and the application process.

search
To apply for a scheme, first identify the scheme that matches your needs using our 'Schemes' section. Once identified, click on 'Apply Now', fill in the required details in the digital form, and upload the necessary documents. You will receive an application ID for tracking.
Eligibility varies by scheme. Generally, it depends on factors like annual income, age, residence (state/district), and category (e.g., student, farmer, artisan). Each scheme page has a dedicated 'Eligibility' tab with detailed requirements.
